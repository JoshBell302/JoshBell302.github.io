Welcome!

Inspired by the classic Mario Party games, this real-life version brings the chaos, luck, and fun to your living room! Use interactive spinning wheels, randomized chance events, and creative mini-games to compete against your friends in a battle for stars and glory.

How to Play

  • 🎲 Players take turns spinning the board wheel to move forward.
  • ⭐ Collect coins and try to earn stars by landing on special spaces.
  • 🎯 After each round, play a mini-game (real-life or digital) to earn bonus coins.
  • ❓ Land on "Chance Time" to trigger random events that can turn the tide of the game.
  • 🏆 The player with the most stars at the end wins — but watch out for surprise twists!
